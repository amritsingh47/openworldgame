🎯 Vision

Create an expansive, immersive open-world experience that gives players complete freedom to explore, interact, and shape their own adventure

Seamless world exploration with dynamic weather, day-night cycles, and AI-driven characters that respond to player choices

Optimized for smooth gameplay across various hardware configurations while maintaining high visual fidelity

✨ Key Features

🌅 Dynamic Environment

Real-time day-night cycles, weather systems, and seasonal changes that affect gameplay

🤖 Intelligent AI

NPCs with behavior trees that respond naturally to player actions and environmental changes

📜 Branching Narratives

Quest systems with multiple outcomes based on player choices and play style

🎮 Seamless Exploration

No loading screens, procedural content generation, and physics-based interactions

Solution: Implemented robust serialization with data integrity checks and corruption recovery
